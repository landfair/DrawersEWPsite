Why Drawers?

Browser bookmarks only go so far. Designed for casual web browsing, rather than professional knowledge management, they fall short in several critical ways:

  • Chaotic Organization: Bookmarks pile up in folders with no clear structure.
  • No Context: You can't add descriptions or explanations about why a resource matters.
  • No Tagging or Filtering: You're stuck with rigid folder hierarchies.

How Drawers Solves This

Drawers reimagines bookmarking as a contextual knowledge management system designed for knowledge workers.

Privacy Policy

Drawers is designed with your privacy as a top priority. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled.

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Your drawers, cards, tags, and all other information never leave your device. This means your data is private and under your control at all times.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or usage data. There are no analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party services that monitor your activity. What you create in Drawers stays on your device.

No Account Required

Drawers works entirely in your browser without requiring an account, login, or any personal information. You maintain complete control over your data without having to trust it to any external service.

Chrome Extension Sync

Important: The Chrome extension and standalone web versions handle data differently:

  • Standalone Web Version: All data stays purely local in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is transmitted.
  • Chrome Extension: Uses Chrome's built-in sync service to synchronize your drawers across devices where you're signed into Chrome.

When using the Chrome extension, if your data is under 100KB total (and each drawer under 8KB), it's transmitted through Google's Chrome Sync infrastructure‚Äînot our servers. We do not have access to your synced data. For larger collections exceeding Chrome's sync limits, data remains local-only on each device. You can disable Chrome sync in your browser settings if you prefer local-only storage.

Export and Delete

You can export your drawers at any time using the download feature in the options menu. You can also delete all your data instantly using the "Reset All Data" option. Your data, your choice.

Your data stays under your control, always.
